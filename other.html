Space Colonies

Space Colonies is a board game finalized during the course TDA580 - Gameplay Design in late 2019. The game features a unique combination of resource management, board control, and mass unit movement, aspiring to emulate the experience of a digital RTS game such as StarCraft. The gameplay was based upon an initial game pitch of mine, but the final results, including art assets, playtesting, report writing, etc., were a joint effort of: me, Tove Ekman, Alexander Nordgren, Lujia Peng, Gustav Svensson, and Simeng Wang. The final presentation scored 10/10 and the final written report scored 15/15.

Magic: The Gathering - Cube
